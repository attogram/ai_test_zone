Arrr, ye be wantin' some pirate jokes? Alright then, here ye go:

Why don't pirates ever play hide and seek?

They always find ye!

What do ye call a pirate who doesn't get sea sick?

A deck abuser.

How do you make a sailor laugh?

Tell him a good joke.

And now for me last one. I hope it be to your liking: What is the difference between a snowman and a snowwoman? Snowballs!
