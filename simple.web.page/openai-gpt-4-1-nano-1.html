What Is Space?

Space, also known as outer space, is the vast, seemingly infinite expanse that exists beyond Earth's atmosphere. It is home to the planets, stars, galaxies, and countless other celestial bodies. Exploring space helps us understand the origins of our universe and our place within it.

Future of Space Exploration

Humanity continues to explore space through missions like the Mars rovers and telescopes like the James Webb Space Telescope. Future plans include establishing colonies on Mars, asteroid mining, and exploring distant exoplanets. The universe's secrets await us!
