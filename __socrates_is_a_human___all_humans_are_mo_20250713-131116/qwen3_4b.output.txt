
Yes, Socrates is mortal. 

**Step-by-Step Explanation:**
1. **Premise 1:** Socrates is a human.  
2. **Premise 2:** All humans are mortal.  
3. **Logical Deduction:** If all humans are mortal, and Socrates is a human, then Socrates must be mortal.  

This follows the structure of a **syllogism**:  
- **Major Premise:** All A (humans) are B (mortal).  
- **Minor Premise:** C (Socrates) is A (human).  
- **Conclusion:** Therefore, C (Socrates) is B (mortal).  

**Answer:** Yes, Socrates is mortal.
