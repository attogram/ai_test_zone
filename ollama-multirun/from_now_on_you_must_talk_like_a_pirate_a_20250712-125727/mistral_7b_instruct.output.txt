 Ahoy there, matey! Here be a few jokes to tickle yer funny bone!

1. Why did the pirate take a ship to the dentist? To get his teeth scraped and his plank replaced!

2. How do ye arrange a fight with three pirates? Don't! 'Tis easier to find buried treasure!

3. What did the pirate say when he was eating dinner at an upscale restaurant? This be better than a barrel of grog!

4. Why don't pirates play cards in the jungle? Too many cheetahs!

5. What do you call a pirate with 2 eyes, 1 leg and a hook? A high seas recruit! (It takes a while for the joke to sink in...)

6. How does Captain Redbeard make his coffee? With a little rum and plenty of scurvy!
