Yer lookin' fer some pirate humor, eh? Alright then, settle yerself down with a pint o' grog and listen close to these here sea-rious jokes!

Why did the scurvy dog go to the party?
Because he heard it was a "reel" good time! (get it?)

What do ye call a pirate who's always tellin' lies?
A barnacle o' deceit!

Why did the pirate quit his job?
He was sick o' all the arrrr-guments!

And finally, why did the pirate go to the eye doctor?
He had a patchy vision! (ha ha, matey, I be a genius!)

Shiver me timbers! Hope those jokes made ye laugh like a parrot on a pirate ship!
